2.1. My Oscillation of Life

The two maps below separately show how my latitude and longitude change over time. According to the map, there is a daily wave in weekdays. That is because I go to CMU every day for school. CMU is famous for its high workload. Thus, my life is quite a "home, school" routine --- but still enjoyable. In weekends, I love to walk around my house or sleep at home, but seldom go to school. Since I don't have a car, it is not very easy to travel far away. Because of that, my activity diameter for weekends is really small.

2.2. Track Me in a Map

Below are showing my traces in maps. According to the heat map, the two places that I appear most frequently are CMU and my house in Greenfield. I take 61C or 61D for school every day. Because of that, there is a trace on Murray and Forves Avenue overlapping the bus routes. Also, I have been to Aldi in Waterfont, a restaurant at North Craig Street, Giant Eagle near my house, Carnegie Library, etc. in the past few days. These places are all shown in the maps.

In the second map, yellow dots represent my location from 0 am to 9 am. Green dots are from 9 am to 7 pm. Purple dots are from 7 pm to 0 am. We can clearly see that from 9 am to 7 pm I am often at school. In other time, I am more likely to be at home.
